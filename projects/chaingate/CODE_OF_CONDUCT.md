# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of identity, background, experience, or role.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, specific, and respectful.
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Protect private, security-sensitive, and personal information.
- Accept responsibility, correct mistakes, and prioritize community safety.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, doxxing, sexualized conduct, deliberate disruption, and publishing another person's private information are not permitted.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers. Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or ban contributors based on severity and pattern. Enforcement decisions should be documented privately and applied consistently.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
